all about donating the needy things to poor people

"Giving is not just about making a donation it's about making a difference"

A donation may take various forms

including money, services or goods such as clothing, food, or vehicles

Charitable donations of goods or services are also called gifts in kind.

Our main aim is to help the poor people and

the area which is effected

by the flood or needs the financial aid.
